Frequently Asked Questions

Real questions from our community, answered by our experts

Are these courses worth it for someone with no tech background?

+

Our courses are designed for beginners and career changers. We start with fundamentals and build up gradually. Many of our most successful students came from non-tech backgrounds like marketing, finance, and retail. The key is our practical, hands-on approach that focuses on real skills rather than just theory.

How do these compare to free YouTube tutorials or Udemy courses?

+

Great question! While free resources are valuable, our courses offer structured learning paths, live mentorship, project reviews, and career guidance. You get personalized feedback, industry connections, and a supportive community. Think of it as the difference between reading a cookbook and learning from a chef who guides you through each step.

What's the job market like for these skills in 2024-2025?

+

The demand is incredibly strong! AI-powered roles, technical product management, and testing automation are among the fastest-growing fields. Companies are desperately seeking professionals who can bridge technical and business domains. Our alumni report 40-80% salary increases within 6 months of completion.

Can I really learn this while working full-time?

+

Yes! Our courses are designed for working professionals. Most students dedicate 8-10 hours per week, with flexible scheduling. We provide recorded sessions, weekend office hours, and asynchronous support. About 85% of our students complete while working full-time.

What if I don't get a job after completing the course?

+

We're confident in our outcomes, but we also provide ongoing career support. This includes resume reviews, interview prep, portfolio guidance, and access to our hiring partner network. We've seen 92% of dedicated students land relevant roles within 6 months. Plus, the skills you learn are valuable regardless of job outcomes.

Is the AI hype real or just a bubble? Should I invest time learning it?

+

AI is transforming every industry, not just tech. It's similar to how the internet changed everything in the 90s. The professionals who learned web development early had massive advantages. Today, AI literacy is becoming as essential as computer literacy was 20 years ago. The question isn't whether to learn it, but how quickly you can start.

How hands-on are these courses? Do I build real projects?

+

Extremely hands-on! Each course includes 3-5 portfolio projects that you can showcase to employers. For example, in Full-Stack Angular Developer with AI Integration, you'll build a complete SaaS application with AI features. In Technical Product Management, you'll create actual product roadmaps and technical specifications for real companies.

What's the difference between your waitlist courses and available ones?

+

Our available courses are ready to start immediately with full curriculum and mentor support. Waitlist courses are in high demand with current batches at capacity. When you join a waitlist, you get priority access to the next batch, early-bird pricing, and exclusive prep materials. These courses typically launch within 2-4 weeks of reaching capacity.
