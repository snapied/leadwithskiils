Educator - Technical Skills

Full-time Dehradun/Remote 2-4 years experience

Create and deliver world-class educational content at Lead With Skills. Design curriculum, conduct live sessions, and mentor students in their career transformation journey in AI, Web Development, and Testing.

About the Role

As a Technical Educator at Lead With Skills, you'll be responsible for creating engaging, practical educational content that transforms careers. You'll work directly with students, design curriculum, and deliver live sessions that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. This role is perfect for someone passionate about education and technology.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and develop comprehensive curriculum for technical courses
  • Conduct live online sessions, workshops, and mentoring sessions
  • Create engaging educational content including videos, assignments, and projects
  • Provide personalized feedback and guidance to students
  • Stay updated with latest industry trends and technologies
  • Collaborate with the content team to improve course materials
  • Assess student progress and adapt teaching methods accordingly
  • Participate in curriculum reviews and educational strategy discussions

Requirements

  • 2-4 years of experience in education, training, or technical instruction
  • Strong expertise in one or more areas: AI/ML, Web Development, Software Testing
  • Experience with online teaching platforms and educational technology
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Passion for teaching and mentoring students
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • Industry certifications in relevant technologies preferred
  • Experience with curriculum design and educational content creation

Technical Skills

  • AI/ML: Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Machine Learning algorithms
  • Web Development: JavaScript, React, Node.js, HTML/CSS, databases
  • Testing: Manual testing, Automation tools, API testing, Performance testing
  • Tools: Git, Docker, Cloud platforms (AWS/Azure), CI/CD
  • Educational Tools: LMS platforms, video editing, presentation software

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary with performance bonuses
  • Flexible work arrangements and remote work options
  • Professional development budget for courses and certifications
  • Access to latest tools and technologies
  • Opportunity to shape the future of technical education
  • Collaborative environment with industry experts
  • Health insurance and wellness benefits
  • Direct impact on student career transformations
