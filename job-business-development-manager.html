Business Development Manager

Full-time Dehradun/Remote 3-5 years experience

Drive strategic partnerships and business growth at Lead With Skills. Work with educational institutions, corporates, and industry leaders to expand our reach and transform careers across India.

About the Role

As a Business Development Manager at Lead With Skills, you'll be at the forefront of our growth strategy. You'll identify, develop, and manage strategic partnerships that drive our mission to transform careers through skill-based education. This role is perfect for someone who thrives in a fast-paced environment and wants to make a meaningful impact in the EdTech space.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify and develop strategic partnerships with educational institutions, corporates, and industry leaders
  • Create and execute business development strategies to expand market reach
  • Build and maintain relationships with key stakeholders and decision-makers
  • Negotiate partnership agreements and contracts
  • Collaborate with internal teams to ensure successful partnership implementation
  • Analyze market trends and identify new business opportunities
  • Represent Lead With Skills at industry events and conferences
  • Achieve quarterly and annual business development targets

Requirements

  • 3-5 years of experience in B2B sales, business development, or partnership management
  • Proven track record of achieving sales targets and building strategic partnerships
  • Experience in the EdTech, education, or technology industry preferred
  • Strong communication, negotiation, and presentation skills
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, or related field
  • Proficiency in CRM tools and business development software
  • Willingness to travel as needed for client meetings and industry events

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary with performance-based incentives
  • Flexible work arrangements (remote/hybrid options available)
  • Professional development budget for courses and conferences
  • Health insurance and wellness benefits
  • Opportunity to work with industry experts and thought leaders
  • Direct impact on transforming careers across India
  • Collaborative and inclusive work environment
  • Stock options and equity participation
