Content Writer

Full-time Remote 1-3 years experience

Craft compelling educational content, course materials, and marketing copy at Lead With Skills. Help communicate our mission and create engaging learning experiences that transform careers.

About the Role

As a Content Writer at Lead With Skills, you'll be the voice behind our educational content and marketing materials. You'll create compelling, clear, and engaging content that helps students understand complex technical concepts and motivates them to transform their careers. This role is perfect for someone who loves writing, has a passion for education, and wants to make a meaningful impact.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create engaging course content, lesson materials, and educational resources
  • Write compelling marketing copy for website, social media, and campaigns
  • Develop blog posts, case studies, and thought leadership articles
  • Collaborate with educators to translate technical concepts into accessible content
  • Optimize content for SEO and user engagement
  • Create email campaigns and newsletter content
  • Maintain brand voice and consistency across all content
  • Research industry trends and incorporate insights into content strategy

Requirements

  • 1-3 years of experience in content writing, preferably in EdTech or technology
  • Strong portfolio showcasing educational or technical content
  • Excellent command of English with impeccable grammar and style
  • Understanding of SEO principles and content optimization
  • Ability to simplify complex technical concepts for diverse audiences
  • Bachelor's degree in English, Journalism, Communications, or related field
  • Experience with content management systems and marketing tools
  • Strong research skills and attention to detail

Content Areas

  • Educational Content: Course materials, lesson plans, assignments, quizzes
  • Marketing Content: Website copy, landing pages, email campaigns
  • Blog Content: Industry insights, career advice, technical tutorials
  • Social Media: Posts, captions, engagement content
  • Documentation: User guides, FAQs, help articles

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary with performance bonuses
  • Fully remote work with flexible hours
  • Professional development budget for writing courses and tools
  • Access to premium writing and design tools
  • Opportunity to build a diverse content portfolio
  • Collaborative environment with educators and marketers
  • Health insurance and wellness benefits
  • Direct impact on student learning experiences
