Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs and services.

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any of our courses by visiting the Courses page and clicking "Join Cohort" on your preferred program. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process.

What is the mentorship program?

Our mentorship program connects you with industry experts from top companies. You'll get 1-on-1 guidance, career advice, and insider knowledge to accelerate your professional growth.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. Please check our refund policy page for detailed information about our refund terms and conditions.

How long does it take to complete a course?

Course duration varies by program. Most of our courses are designed to be completed in 8-12 weeks with 5-10 hours of weekly commitment, including live sessions and assignments.

Are the courses suitable for beginners?

We offer courses for all skill levels. Each course page clearly indicates the prerequisite knowledge required, and we provide foundational resources for beginners.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, upon successful completion of any course, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume.
