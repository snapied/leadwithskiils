Who we are and what's our story

In 2020, we built 'Design Sundays', a big meetup community where designers could connect, share ideas, and learn from each other. These sessions were a hit.

But we noticed something: people kept coming with the same questions and needed guidance and actionable advice.

We wanted to fix that. So we took the leap. We transformed offline meetups into online courses, workshops, and cohort accessible to anyone, anywhere.

Our courses are designed to give you practical insights, real-world tips, and clear steps. Think of it as learning from someone who's already walked the path you're on.

No fancy jargon here, no outdated lectures. Just real-world insights and skills you can actually use. That's when Lead With Skills was born.

Today, we're more than a community. We're a platform helping thousands of learners gain the skills they need to succeed.

And we're just getting started!
